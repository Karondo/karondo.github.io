Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned?

Most experts recommend professional carpet cleaning every 12-18 months. However, if you have pets, children, or high foot traffic, you may need cleaning every 6-12 months.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after cleaning?

With our advanced equipment, carpets typically dry in 4-6 hours. We can speed up drying time with industrial air movers upon request.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Absolutely! We use only eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are certified safe for pets and children. All products are biodegradable and hypoallergenic.

Can you remove old, set-in stains?

In most cases, yes! Our advanced stain removal techniques can eliminate even old, set-in stains. We have a 95% success rate on stains that other cleaners couldn't remove.
