Frequently Asked Questions

What cleaning method do you use for carpets?

For carpets, we start by evenly spraying a cleaning solution across the surface. Then we use our EDIC Powermate 1204ACH powered carpet wand, which connects to the Tornado Apartment Pro Recovery Carpet Extractor. The wand’s fast-spinning brush scrubs deep into the fibers to loosen dirt and grime, while the extractor pulls up dirty water and solution, leaving the carpet clean and fresh. This combination removes dirt that regular vacuuming can’t reach.

What cleaning method do you use for upholestry?

For upholstery like couches and chairs, we use the Tornado Portable Carpet Spotter. We spray a cleaning solution onto the fabric and gently agitate it to lift dirt from the fibers. The spotter then vacuums up the dirty water and solution, leaving the upholstery clean, refreshed, and with stains minimized. Its small size and powerful suction makes it ideal for getting deep into furniture fabrics.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Absolutely! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for both children and pets. Your health and safety are our top priority, and we ensure that our products are effective yet gentle on your home environment.

Can professional carpet cleaning remove pet stains and odors?

Pet stains and odors can be tough, but our process tackles them effectively. We pre-treat the area with safe enzyme-based solutions that break down stains and neutralize odors. Using powered wands and extractors for carpets or portable spotters for upholstery, we lift dirt and residues while extracting the solution, leaving surfaces fresh and free of lingering odors.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after professional cleaning?

Carpets usually take between 4–10 hours to dry, depending on the carpet type, level of soiling, and the humidity in your home. Opening windows or using fans can help speed up the process.
