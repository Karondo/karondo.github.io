Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We typically offer same-day or next-day appointments depending on availability. After you request a quote, we'll provide available time slots.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Yes! We use only eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are certified safe for pets and children. All products are biodegradable and hypoallergenic.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after cleaning?

With our advanced equipment, carpets typically dry in 4-6 hours. We can speed up drying time with industrial air movers upon request.

Do you offer commercial cleaning services?

Absolutely! We provide cleaning services for offices, retail spaces, and other commercial properties. Contact us for a customized commercial quote.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before your scheduled service time with no penalty.
